The Car Rental System streamline the process of renting vehicles,


Cusomer: 
    •	Register an account and update personal information.
	•	Log in securely and manage profile settings.
    •	Search for available vehicles based on preferences (date, type, location).
	•	Make reservations and specify pickup and drop-off locations.
    •	Contact support for assistance with booking, billing, or vehicle issues.
Car manager:
    •	Add, update, and remove vehicle information in the system.
	•	Schedule and track vehicle maintenance and servicing.
    •	View, approve, modify, or cancel customer reservations.
	•	Manage vehicle availability and booking schedules.
    •	Process payments and manage billing.
	•	Handle refunds and adjust invoices when needed.
